CinemaNinja Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CinemaNinja! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our movie discovery and social platform.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address and username for account creation
  • Profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Social connections and friend lists

Usage Data:

  • Movie ratings, reviews, and recommendations
  • Search history and content preferences
  • App usage analytics and performance data

Location Data (New in Version 3.0):

  • Country/region detection via IP address for content personalization
  • Manually selected region preferences stored locally
  • No GPS or precise location data is collected

Device Information:

  • Device type, operating system version
  • App version and performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs for improvement

How We Use Your Information

Core Features:

  • Provide personalized movie and TV show recommendations
  • Enable social features like friend connections and clan participation
  • Maintain your watchlists, ratings, and reviews

Location Personalization:

  • Show content available in your region
  • Display local trending movies and shows
  • Provide region-specific streaming availability
  • Cultural content discovery and recommendations

Service Improvement:

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Develop new features based on user preferences
  • Optimize app performance and fix bugs

Communication:

  • Send important app updates and security notices
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Notify about new features (with your consent)

Location Data Handling

Automatic Detection:

  • We detect your country using IP geolocation (via ipapi.co)
  • Only country-level information is obtained, never precise location
  • This enables region-appropriate content recommendations

User Control:

  • You can manually select or change your region anytime
  • Disable location features entirely in Settings
  • Your preference is stored securely on your device

Third-Party Integration:

  • Region information is shared with TMDB for content filtering
  • No personal data is shared beyond country code
  • All data transfers are encrypted and secure

Children's Privacy:

  • Location features are disabled for users under 13
  • Parental consent required for location personalization
  • Default to global content without regional filtering

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database) for movie/TV content and metadata
  • ipapi.co for privacy-friendly country detection
  • Firebase for authentication and cloud storage
  • Analytics services for app improvement (anonymized data only)

Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose information if required by law
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when legally obligated
  • Protection of our rights and user safety

Business Transfers:

  • In case of merger or acquisition, user data may be transferred
  • Users will be notified of any ownership changes
  • Same privacy protections will apply

Data Security

Protection Measures:

  • All data transmitted is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure cloud storage with regular security audits
  • Access controls limit who can view your information

Account Security:

  • Strong password requirements and optional two-factor authentication
  • Secure session management and automatic logout
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments

Breach Response:

  • Immediate investigation and containment of any security incidents
  • User notification within 72 hours of confirmed breaches
  • Cooperation with authorities when required

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access:

  • Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • View and update your profile information anytime
  • Export your ratings, reviews, and watchlists

Data Control:

  • Delete specific content (reviews, ratings, posts)
  • Adjust privacy settings for social features
  • Control notification preferences

Account Management:

  • Temporarily deactivate your account
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Download your data before deletion

Location Features:

  • Enable or disable location-based personalization
  • Change your region selection anytime
  • Reset location preferences to default

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Age Requirements:

  • CinemaNinja is intended for users 13 years and older
  • Users under 13 require parental consent
  • Special protections for users under 18

Parental Controls:

  • Parents can review and delete their child's information
  • Opt-out of data collection for children
  • Control social features and interactions

Limited Data Collection:

  • Minimal data collection for users under 13
  • No behavioral advertising to children
  • No location tracking for underage users

International Users

Global Service:

  • CinemaNinja is available worldwide with regional content
  • Data may be processed in different countries
  • All transfers comply with applicable privacy laws

GDPR Compliance (EU Users):

  • Lawful basis for processing your data
  • Right to data portability and erasure
  • Data Protection Officer contact: dpo@cinemaninja.app

CCPA Compliance (California Users):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (note: we don't sell data)

Data Retention

Account Data:

  • Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Some data may be retained for legal or safety reasons

Location Preferences:

  • Stored until you change the setting or delete the app
  • Automatically cleared when you log out
  • No long-term server storage of location data

Analytics Data:

  • Anonymized usage data retained for up to 2 years
  • Crash reports and performance data for 1 year
  • Legal compliance data as required by law

Changes to This Policy

Updates:

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • Material changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes

Notification:

  • Email notification for significant privacy changes
  • In-app notification of policy updates
  • Previous versions available upon request
